Welcome, players, to the Queen of Hearts! Here, the manicured lawn of a croquet field hides a far more treacherous game. Forget your mallet skills; your true weapon is your heart.

The rules are simple, yet the challenge is profound: convince your fellow players that love, empathy, and genuine connection are paramount for survival. Forge alliances, share your vulnerabilities, and prove that humanity can triumph even in the face of death.

But be warned: deception lurks beneath every smile. Can you discern genuine bonds from manipulative strategies? Can you sway the hearts of others, or will you become another casualty of this twisted game?

Each of you starts with a fragile hope. Nurture it, defend it, and let it guide you. The Queen is watching, and only those who truly believe in the power of love will survive.

Let the game begin!
