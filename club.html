Welcome to the King of Clubs.

This is no sanctuary and no test of chance. The arena itself shifts like a puzzle with no solution, pressing you to think faster and move sharper than those around you. Every choice carries weight, and every mistake leaves a scar that cannot be undone.

Survival here demands more than courage. You must read every expression, anticipate every move, and shape the game before it shapes you. Trust may appear as a weapon, but it is fragile, ready to break when you need it most. Even the closest ally can become your executioner when the moment turns.

The King does not care for kindness, nor does he reward brute strength. He rewards precision, calculation, and the ability to twist chaos into a path forward. The question is not if you will play, but how long you can endure.

The game begins now. Outsmart them all, or be consumed by the board itself.
