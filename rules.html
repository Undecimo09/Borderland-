SURVIVAL RULES

Understanding these rules is the difference between life and death in the Borderland. Memorize them. Your life depends on it.

WARNING: Failure to follow these rules will result in immediate elimination. There are no second chances in the Borderland.

01: Game Registration

All players must register for games before participation. Missing registration means immediate elimination.

02: Visa Expiration

Every player has a visa with limited days. Failing to clear games before expiration results in laser elimination.

03: Game Completion

Once a game begins, all players must see it through to completion. There is no exit until the game ends.

04: Playing Cards

Clear games to collect playing cards. Different suits represent different game types and difficulties.

05: Face Cards

Face cards (Jack, Queen, King, Ace) represent the most dangerous games. Approach with extreme caution.

06: Safe Zones

The Beach and other safe zones provide temporary refuge.
